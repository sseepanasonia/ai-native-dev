Company Benefits and Leave Policy

Employee Benefits:
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) with 5% company match
- Flexible spending accounts
- Life insurance
- Disability insurance
- Employee assistance program

Leave Policy:
- Vacation: 15 days per year (increases with tenure)
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Personal days: 3 days per year
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid
- Bereavement leave: 5 days

Professional Development:
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: 1-2 per year
- Certification reimbursement
- Mentorship program

Work-Life Balance:
- Flexible work hours
- Remote work options
- Wellness programs
- Employee social events
